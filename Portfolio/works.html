Proposition for new movie: Tron(2023)
In this project, I decided to use a technique known as low poly to present an upcoming film. I did over 800 shapes, added a gradient background and the tron film logo with a light blue text shadow.
Proposition for Bad Boy Perfume
For this project I decided to make lightning stand out as a way to call out a well known perfume, Bad Boy (Carolina Herrera). The lightning itself rerpresents is the form of the perfume bottle. I used the technique bevel and emboss on the mans face to add the lightning as if the perfume makes him shine and stand out. the dark background helps focus on the lightning which is the main objective in this project.
Proposition for La Sportiva

¨Walking is a man's best medicine¨

The main focus of the company, ¨La Sportiva¨, is the production of hiking footwear. In my project, I placed 2 pictures in a polar coordinate, indicating day and night. I also added mountains and all kinds of weather and terrains to express the fact that the shoes can last through anything.
Advertisement for apple for Iphone 6

¨Forget about the Iphone 5, Get ready for the Iphone 6¨

In this project, I strived to do the dispersion effect on an Iphone 5. In my opinion, the Iphone 6 was the biggest change the company of Apple did, and so I decided to do an advertisement in which the Iphone 5 faded away, bringing in the Iphone 6. I also added the apple logo dispersing out of the phone. The nature in the background is intended to act as if the iphone is an apple falling from a tree, which is why i added apples on the ground and falling.
